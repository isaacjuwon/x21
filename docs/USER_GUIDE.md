## Loans

Apply for loans based on your share holdings.

### Loan Levels

Your loan level determines your maximum loan amount:

| Level | Repayments Required | Benefits |
|-------|---------------------|----------|
| **Basic** | 0 | Entry level |
| **Silver** | 5 successful repayments | Higher loan limits |
| **Gold** | 15 successful repayments | Better terms |
| **Platinum** | 30 successful repayments | Maximum benefits |

**Automatic Upgrades:** Your level upgrades automatically as you make successful repayments!

### Applying for a Loan

1. Go to **Loans** â†’ **Apply for Loan**
2. View your eligibility:
   - Current loan level
   - Maximum loan amount
   - Required shares
3. Enter desired loan amount
4. Select loan duration (months)
5. Review:
   - Monthly payment
   - Interest rate
   - Total repayment
6. Click "Submit Application"
7. Wait for approval (instant for eligible amounts)

### Loan Eligibility

To qualify for a loan:
- âœ… Must have sufficient shares (loan-to-shares ratio)
- âœ… No active/pending loans
- âœ… Amount within your loan level limit
- âœ… Loan level assigned

### Making Loan Payments

1. Navigate to **Loans** â†’ **My Loans**
2. Click on active loan
3. Click "Make Payment"
4. Enter payment amount (minimum: monthly payment)
5. Confirm payment
6. Payment deducted from wallet

**Payment Tips:**
- Pay on or before due date to avoid penalties
- You can pay more than the monthly amount
- Early repayment reduces total interest
- Each successful payment counts toward level upgrade

### Viewing Loan Details

Access your loan to view:
- Loan amount and balance
- Interest rate
- Monthly payment
- Payment schedule
- Payment history
- Next due date
